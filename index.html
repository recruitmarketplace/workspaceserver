FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting your server.

What is Workspace Server?
Workspace Server provides dedicated virtual private servers specifically built for hosting MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5 trading algorithms. We handle the infrastructure — provisioning, uptime, monitoring, and maintenance — so your Expert Advisor runs 24/7 on reliable, enterprise-grade hardware without depending on your home internet or personal computer.
Which MetaTrader versions are supported?
Both MetaTrader 4 (MT4) and MetaTrader 5 (MT5) are fully supported. Your server comes with your chosen platform pre-installed and configured. If you need both installed simultaneously, that's supported as well — the server has enough resources to handle multiple terminal instances.
How do I connect to my server?
You connect via Remote Desktop Protocol (RDP), which is built into every Windows computer and available as a free app on Mac, iOS, and Android. We provide your login credentials after provisioning. You'll have full desktop access to your server — just like sitting in front of a Windows computer, from anywhere in the world.
How quickly is my server provisioned?
Most servers are provisioned and ready within a few hours of payment. Yearly plan subscribers receive priority provisioning. You'll receive an email with your server IP address, login credentials, and a quick-start guide as soon as your server is live.
What happens if the server goes down?
Our infrastructure is built with redundant power, network, and storage systems. In the rare event of a hardware failure, automatic failover systems restore your server quickly. We monitor all servers 24/7 and proactively address issues before they impact your uptime. Our 99.9% SLA is backed by our commitment to reliability.
Can I run multiple Expert Advisors on one server?
Yes. With 4 dedicated vCPU cores and 8 GB of RAM, your server can comfortably handle multiple Expert Advisors and even multiple MetaTrader instances simultaneously. The dedicated resources ensure consistent performance regardless of how many EAs you're running.
What brokers are compatible?
Workspace Server is compatible with any broker that supports MetaTrader 4 or MetaTrader 5. You choose your own broker, open your own account, and connect your terminal. We are an infrastructure provider — we don't recommend, endorse, or have any affiliation with any brokerage.
How does renewal pricing work?
After your initial term (monthly or yearly), both plans renew at our loyalty rate of $291/year — that's 3x the monthly rate, billed annually. This comes out to just $24.25/month. We believe in rewarding long-term clients, not surprising them with price increases.
